Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ Categories

What is FlowerBTC?

FlowerBTC is a revolutionary crypto ecosystem that combines GameFi, DeFi, and AI-powered trading. Our platform features a unique sunflower growth game where users can earn rewards, trade tokens, and participate in a vibrant community.

How do I get started with FlowerBTC?

Getting started is easy:

  1. Join our Telegram bot
  2. Connect your wallet
  3. Start growing your sunflower
  4. Earn rewards and trade $FBTC
Is FlowerBTC safe and secure?

Yes! Security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption, secure smart contracts audited by third parties, and follow best practices for data protection. Your assets are always under your control.

How can I contact support?

You can reach our support team through:

  • Email: support@flowerbtc.io
  • Telegram: @FlowerBTC_community
  • Discord: FlowerBTC Server
How does the sunflower growth game work?

Your sunflower grows through 10 levels as you play. Collect Yellow Sun tokens, use Magical Potions to boost growth, and Red Sun items for maximum upgrades. Higher levels unlock better rewards and features.

What are Yellow Sun tokens?

Yellow Sun is the main in-game currency. You earn it by playing the game, completing tasks, and participating in community events. Use it to buy potions, upgrade your sunflower, and trade for other items.

How do I level up my sunflower?

Level up by:

  • Collecting Yellow Sun tokens
  • Using Magical Potions (purchased with Yellow Sun)
  • Using Red Sun items for major upgrades
  • Completing daily tasks and challenges
What rewards can I earn?

Rewards include:

  • Yellow Sun tokens
  • $FBTC tokens
  • NFT collectibles
  • Exclusive badges and achievements
  • Bonus multipliers for staking
What is $FBTC?

$FBTC is the native utility and governance token of the FlowerBTC ecosystem. It powers our GameFi mechanics, reward farming, and AI-powered trading bots. Holders can stake, vote on proposals, and earn passive income.

How can I buy $FBTC?

$FBTC will be available on major decentralized exchanges. You can also earn $FBTC through:

  • Playing the sunflower game
  • Staking rewards
  • Community airdrops
  • Referral bonuses
Can I stake $FBTC?

Yes! Staking $FBTC allows you to earn passive income while supporting the ecosystem. Different staking tiers offer varying rewards and benefits. Check the staking dashboard for current APY rates.

What are the tokenomics?

Token distribution:

  • 40% - Community rewards & airdrops
  • 25% - Ecosystem development
  • 15% - Team & advisors (vested)
  • 10% - Liquidity provision
  • 10% - Marketing & partnerships
Which wallets are supported?

We support major Web3 wallets including:

  • MetaMask
  • WalletConnect
  • Trust Wallet
  • Coinbase Wallet
  • Rainbow
Which blockchain does FlowerBTC use?

FlowerBTC is built on multiple blockchains for optimal performance and accessibility. Our primary chain is Ethereum (Layer 2 for lower fees), with cross-chain expansion planned for Phase 4.

Are the smart contracts audited?

Yes, all smart contracts undergo rigorous auditing by reputable third-party security firms. Audit reports are publicly available on our documentation portal.

How do I report a bug?

Report bugs through:

  • Our Discord #bug-reports channel
  • Email: bugs@flowerbtc.io
  • GitHub Issues (for technical users)
